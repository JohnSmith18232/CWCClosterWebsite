About us

Our Mission Our Events Testimonials

Your pup deserves the best!

Our team at Canine Wellness Center Closter is committed to establishing a trusting relationship with our clients and their pups that last a lifetime. We believe in communicating your dogs' needs with you during each visit to ensure your pup gets all they need and deserve. We understand not all dogs love a spa day, so it's very important for us to do what it takes to deliver the most pleasant experience to ensure your pup is happy after every visit. We are proud to have established our clientele mostly by word of mouth since 2007 and are excited to share with you our new and improved website with you. We hope to keep growing our Canine Wellness Center family, while still providing our senior pups with the utmost service.

Our History

Jan Rivero, founder and owner of Jan’s Dog House and Canine Wellness Center, has been a dog groomer for over 10 years and is certified by the New York School of Dog Grooming (’07). Jan’s connection and engagement with her clients throughout the years with caring, honest and trustworthy service helped her build a big network of clients mostly referred by word of mouth. Her clients' loyalty to Jan’s Dog House has helped her establish a strong and solid reputation.

Our Philosophy

At Canine Wellness Center Closter, your pup's mental and physical well being is our utmost priority. We believe for an easeful grooming experience, your pet must become familiar with grooming while bonding with us in a healthy environment. We will always try our best to accommodate your needs. We are wholeheartedly committed to providing our clients with the information and resources to raise a healthy happy pup.

Why Choose Us?

Your pet’s wellbeing is our top priority so we stay mindful of your pet’s age, personality, and breed, making sure to cater to all personal needs. Whether your dog is 12 weeks/ 12 years old, or outgoing/ shy, we do our best to make grooming a pleasurable experience for all dogs by providing a welcoming, stress- free environment. We provide one on one spa service with lots of love and attention, focusing and interacting with your pup during their entire stay.
